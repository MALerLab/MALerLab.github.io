### H3 Title
We aim to develop a reliable end-to-end pipeline for automatic transcription and alignment of Korean traditional music, spanning diverse instruments and vocal styles. The project includes dataset curation, modality-aligned preprocessing, and training of state-of-the-art models for notation-aware recognition.

Key milestones:

- Dataset consolidation and cleaning across institutions
- On-the-fly augmentation and pitch-aware preprocessing
- Multimodal alignment between audio and symbolic score
- Benchmarking with standard MIR metrics (F1, CER/WER for lyrics, onset/offset F1)
